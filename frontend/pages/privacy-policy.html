Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

Introduction

Welcome to Bharath Medicare. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal health information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our platform.

Your Privacy Matters: We use military-grade encryption to protect your medical records and personal information. You have complete control over who can access your data.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Full name, email address, and phone number
  • Date of birth and gender
  • Address and emergency contact information
  • Blood group and medical history
  • Profile photographs (optional)

Medical Information

  • Medical records and documents you upload
  • Chronic conditions and allergies
  • Current medications
  • Health card information

Professional Information (For Doctors)

  • NMC (National Medical Commission) UID
  • Professional credentials and verification documents

Usage Information

  • Login timestamps and IP addresses
  • Access logs and audit trails
  • Device information and browser type

How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • Healthcare Management: To store and manage your medical records securely
  • Access Control: To facilitate controlled sharing of records with authorized healthcare providers
  • Authentication: To verify your identity and maintain account security
  • Communication: To send important updates about your account and services
  • Compliance: To comply with legal and regulatory requirements
  • Improvement: To improve our services and user experience

Data Security

We implement industry-leading security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption: All medical records are encrypted using AES-256 encryption before storage
  • Secure Transmission: Data is transmitted over secure HTTPS connections
  • Access Controls: Role-based access control ensures only authorized users can view data
  • Audit Logs: Complete audit trails track all access to your medical records
  • Regular Backups: Your data is regularly backed up to prevent loss
  • Security Monitoring: 24/7 monitoring for suspicious activities

Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We only share your information in the following circumstances:

  • With Your Consent: When you grant access to specific doctors or healthcare providers
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect rights and safety
  • Service Providers: With trusted third-party service providers who assist in operating our platform (under strict confidentiality agreements)
You Control Access: You can grant or revoke doctor access to your records at any time through your dashboard.

Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: View all your personal and medical information
  • Correction: Update or correct your information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and data
  • Export: Download your medical records at any time
  • Control: Manage who has access to your records
  • Audit: View complete logs of who accessed your data and when

Cookies and Tracking

We use essential cookies to maintain your session and ensure platform functionality. We do not use tracking cookies for advertising purposes.

Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
