Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions

How do I create an account?

Click on "Register" button, choose your role (Patient/Doctor), fill in your details, and submit. Patients are verified instantly, while doctors need admin approval.

How secure is my medical data?

We use AES-256 encryption for all medical records. Your data is encrypted before storage and transmitted over secure HTTPS connections. Only you and authorized doctors can access your records.

How do I grant access to a doctor?

Go to your dashboard, navigate to "Access Control" section, enter the doctor's email, and click "Grant Access". The doctor will immediately be able to view your records.

What is the QR code on my health card?

The QR code contains your patient ID and can be scanned at hospitals for quick access to your medical records without needing to log in manually.

Can I revoke doctor access?

Yes! You can revoke access at any time from your dashboard. Once revoked, the doctor will no longer be able to view your records.

What file types can I upload?

You can upload PDF, JPG, and PNG files up to 10MB in size. We recommend using PDF for medical reports and images for scans.
