Getting Started

  • How to create an account

    Click the "Register" button on the homepage, select your role (Patient or Doctor), fill in your email, password, and full name. Patients are verified instantly, while doctors need to provide their NMC UID and wait for admin approval.

  • Patient registration guide

    As a patient, you'll need to provide basic information during registration. After creating your account, complete your profile with medical details like blood group, allergies, chronic conditions, and emergency contacts to unlock all features.

  • Doctor registration process

    Doctors must provide their 7-digit NMC (National Medical Commission) UID during registration. Your account will be pending until an admin verifies your credentials. You'll receive access once approved.

  • Completing your profile

    Navigate to your dashboard and click on "Profile" section. Fill in all required fields including personal information, medical history, and emergency contacts. A complete profile is required to access all platform features.

Medical Records

  • Uploading medical records

    Go to "Records" section in your dashboard, click "Upload New Record", select a file (PDF, JPG, or PNG up to 10MB), add a description, and click upload. Your file will be encrypted and stored securely.

  • Viewing your records

    All your uploaded records are displayed in the "Records" section. You can see the file name, upload date, and description. Click on any record to view it in full screen or download it.

  • Downloading records

    Click the download icon next to any record to save it to your device. The file will be decrypted and downloaded in its original format. You can download your records anytime.

  • Deleting records

    Click the delete icon next to a record and confirm the action. Note: Deleted records cannot be recovered. Make sure to download important files before deleting them.

Doctor Access

  • Granting access to doctors

    Navigate to "Access Control" section, enter the doctor's registered email address, and click "Grant Access". The doctor will immediately be able to view all your medical records and profile information.

  • Revoking doctor access

    In the "Access Control" section, find the doctor in your authorized list and click "Revoke". The doctor will lose access immediately and won't be able to view your records anymore.

  • Viewing access history

    Check the "Audit Logs" section to see complete history of who accessed your records, when they accessed them, and what actions they performed. All access is tracked for your security.

  • Managing permissions

    You have complete control over your data. You can grant or revoke access to any doctor at any time. Currently, all authorized doctors have full read access to your records.

Health Card & QR Code

  • Understanding your health card

    Your digital health card contains your patient ID, personal information, blood group, and a QR code. It's available in the "Health Card" section and displays key medical information for quick reference.

  • Using QR code at hospitals

    Show your health card QR code at hospital reception. They can scan it using the hospital portal to instantly access your medical records without you needing to log in manually.

  • Downloading health card

    In the "Health Card" section, click "Download" to save your health card as an image. You can also print it or save it to your phone for easy access at medical facilities.

  • Hospital portal access

    Hospitals can access the dedicated hospital portal to scan patient QR codes. This provides quick, secure access to patient records during emergencies or routine visits without password requirements.

Security & Privacy

  • How we protect your data

    We use military-grade AES-256 encryption for all medical records, secure HTTPS connections for data transmission, and role-based access control. Your data is stored in secure cloud servers with regular backups.

  • Encryption explained

    All your medical files are encrypted before storage using AES-256 encryption. This means even if someone gains unauthorized access to our servers, they cannot read your files without the encryption key.

  • Audit logs and tracking

    Every access to your records is logged with timestamp, user information, and IP address. You can view complete audit trails in your dashboard to see who accessed your data and when.

  • Account security tips

    Use a strong password with at least 8 characters including uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and symbols. Never share your password. Log out after each session, especially on shared devices.

Account Settings

  • Updating profile information

    Go to "Profile" section in your dashboard, click "Edit Profile", update your information (name, phone, address, medical details), and click "Save Changes". Your profile will be updated immediately.

  • Changing password

    Navigate to account settings, enter your current password, then your new password twice for confirmation. Your new password must be at least 8 characters with mixed case, numbers, and symbols.

  • Managing notifications

    In settings, you can control email notifications for account activities, access grants, and system updates. Toggle each notification type on or off based on your preferences.

  • Deactivating account

    Contact support to request account deactivation. Note: Deactivating your account will revoke all doctor access and make your records inaccessible. Download important files before deactivation.
